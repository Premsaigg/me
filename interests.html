Coffee

I've brewed at home since 2021 using a moka pot, Picopresso, and pour-over. The ritual stuck.

I enjoy the balance between experimentation and a reliably good morning cup. The daily ritual feels meditative. I gravitate towards Indian coffees: sweet, low acidity, medium body.

Writing

I write about sports on Substack: fan diaries that connect personal passion with broader narratives.

I follow cycling, tennis, cricket, golf, and rugby closely. What interests me beyond fandom is the machinery behind it: how tournaments are executed, fan experiences crafted, and teams build culture alongside commercial success.

My writing explores these themes through the lens of being a fan. I'm building a regular writing practice.

Reading

I lean towards politics, economics, and policy. Books that help me understand how systems work and why they fail.

I'm drawn to writers who can explain complex systems clearly and show how incentives shape outcomes. The best books challenge my assumptions and show me patterns I hadn't noticed.
